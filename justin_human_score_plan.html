Manual entry pathway — what the patient sees for non-API data

A significant chunk of clinical data (DEXA reports, Grail Galleri results, paper lab results, dental records, CAC scan reports) has no automated API path. The Medesto app needs an upload + extract pipeline.

UI stepWhat the patient doesWhat Medesto does behind the scenes
1. Add dataTap "Add Result" in Health Hub → pick category (Lab / Imaging / Genomic / Other)Routes to category-specific upload flow
2. UploadTake photo of report OR import PDF from FilesFiles uploaded to S3 (encrypted, HIPAA bucket)
3. ExtractWait ~10 sec while AI reads itAWS Textract OCRs the PDF · LLM (Claude or Bedrock) extracts known fields (test name, value, unit, reference range, date)
4. ConfirmPatient reviews extracted fields → taps "Confirm" or editsConfirmed fields written to measurements or new clinical tables; raw PDF stays in S3 for audit
5. Care team review(Optional) Clinician sees the new entry flagged as "patient-reported, AI-extracted"Confidence score attached; clinician can verify before scoring
Manual entry caveat: AI extraction is ~95% accurate for standard lab PDFs but drops for handwritten notes, multi-page reports, and non-standard formats. The "Confirm" step is non-negotiable — the patient (or staff) must accept each extracted value before it flows into the Human Score. Otherwise score quality degrades from one bad scan.
